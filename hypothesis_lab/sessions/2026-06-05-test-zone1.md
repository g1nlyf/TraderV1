## Meta-finding

Every Zone-1 amplifier of H-042 (recency, accumulation, funding-conditioning, breadth, persistence,
sign-flip) **fails to beat plain H-042**, and several invert. The forced-flow bounce is a *single-name,
idiosyncratic* phenomenon: conditioning on "more systemic / more crowded" either adds noise or predicts
continuation. H-077's inversion (low-breadth bounces most) is the sharpest evidence — it argues the edge
is genuine forced-liquidation overshoot in isolated names, contaminated (not amplified) by systemic beta.
H-060 (repeat-cascade) is the only magnitude-positive lead and is purely n-limited — queue for
forward-collection alongside the H-042 1m-entry work. The basis trap (H-065) reaffirms the bid-ask /
non-synchronous-close discipline.
